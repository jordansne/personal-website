About Me

I'm a passionate software developer that loves to focus on developing apps with performance and ease-of-use in mind. I current attend Western University and plan on graduating with an Honurs Specialization in Computer Science with a Minor in Software Engineering.

Over the past years I've gained a lot of my experience from personal projects I've worked on in my spare time. My favourite languages are Java & NodeJS, and while I prefer working on backend areas of applications, I've recently begun exploring full-stack development using the MEAN stack.
